Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Tuvora ("we," "our," or "us") is developed by TDLabs LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Tuvora app ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

Tuvora is designed to work without requiring you to create an account. We collect minimal information necessary to provide the service:

  • Anonymous User ID: When you first open Tuvora, we automatically generate an anonymous identifier using Firebase Authentication. This ID is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information.
  • Usage Data: We store your generation quota (how many images you have generated and your reset date) and credit balance associated with your anonymous ID in our database.
  • Images: Photos you submit for editing are sent to our server to be processed by OpenAI's image API. We do not store your images after processing is complete.
  • Subscription Status: If you purchase a subscription or credit pack, Apple processes the payment and provides us with your subscription status. We do not receive your payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and return AI-edited images based on your prompts
  • To track and enforce your generation quota
  • To manage your subscription tier and credit balance
  • To improve the performance and reliability of the App

3. Images and AI Processing

Photos you submit are transmitted securely to our Firebase Cloud Function, which forwards them to OpenAI's API for processing. Images are used solely to generate the requested output and are not stored on our servers after processing. OpenAI's use of submitted data is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Your anonymous user data (quota, subscription status, credit balance) is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, hosted in the United States. No personally identifiable information is stored.

5. Third-Party Services

Tuvora uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database — Firebase Privacy
  • OpenAI: AI image generation and editing — OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: In-app purchases and subscriptions — Apple Privacy Policy

6. Photos and Camera Access

Tuvora requests access to your camera and photo library solely to allow you to select or capture images for editing. We do not access your photos beyond what you explicitly select and submit. Generated images are automatically saved to your photo library with your permission.

7. Children's Privacy

Tuvora is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data Retention

Your anonymous user record is retained as long as you use the App. If you delete the App, your anonymous record will remain in our database but is not linked to any identifiable information. You may request deletion by contacting us.

9. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your anonymous data at any time by contacting us. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, we cannot identify you by name or email, but we can delete your record if you provide your anonymous user ID (visible in App settings).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
